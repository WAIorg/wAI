Kinect RGB/Depth Alignment Test

How to Use This Test

  • RGB Stream: Shows the color camera feed
  • Depth Stream: Shows the depth camera feed (registered to RGB coordinates)
  • Aligned Stream: Shows RGB and depth overlaid (50% each)
  • Verification: Click to get alignment metrics

Good alignment: Objects in RGB and depth should match pixel-perfectly. The aligned stream should show clear overlay without ghosting.

What to Look For

  • Excellent Alignment: Correlation > 0.7, Overlap > 60%
  • Good Alignment: Correlation > 0.5, Overlap > 40%
  • Fair Alignment: Correlation > 0.3, Overlap > 20%
  • Poor Alignment: Below fair thresholds

Visual Check: In the aligned stream, edges and features should be crisp and well-defined, not blurry or doubled.
